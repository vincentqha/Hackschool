EDscapade Games Project

Built for the adventurer, designed for humans

What is EDscapade?

Having experienced the vibrant escape room culture in Southern California, my friends and I were left dissatisfied with the escape rooms in the Bay Area.


"What if we started our own escape room?" That's when ambition struck at 110%.


This project has been in the works for 2+ years and we launched it the summer of 2020. We designed the games, we built the mechanisms, and we turned an idea into a tangible space where we're able to share our passion with the community.
